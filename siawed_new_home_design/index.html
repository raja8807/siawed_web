About SIAWED

Empowering Women Through Entrepreneurship

Southern Industrial Academy for Women Entrepreneurs Development (SIAWED) is a not-for-profit organisation dedicated to empowering women through entrepreneurship, skill development, leadership and economic opportunities across India.

What We Do

A Complete Ecosystem to Start, Grow & Scale

🚀

Entrepreneurship Development

End-to-end training programs for aspiring and existing women entrepreneurs — from idea validation to business launch and growth.

🎓

Capacity Building & Training

Industry-relevant skill programs to enhance professional and entrepreneurial competencies, including digital and leadership skills.

🛍

Market Access & WENBA

Showcase products through exhibitions, events and the WENBA marketplace — reaching corporate buyers and consumers nationwide.

🏢

Corporate Linkages

Connecting women-owned businesses with corporate procurement teams, investors and strategic partners for sustainable growth.

🌿

Community Outreach

Health, wellness, human rights advocacy and environmental awareness programs that uplift entire communities and families.

🧵

SHG & Artisan Support

Dedicated pathways for self-help groups and native artisans to formalise, brand and scale their craft-based enterprises.

Empowering women to lead, build, and scale their dreams — one enterprise at a time.

— Ms T Vanadhi Devi, Founder & President, SIAWED

Founder Spotlight

14 Years Creating Opportunity, Dignity & Freedom

Led by a first-generation entrepreneur with over 25 years of business leadership experience, SIAWED's founder has dedicated her life to creating economic freedom for women across India — shaping SIAWED into a thriving ecosystem of thousands of entrepreneurs.

  1. Founded SIAWED in 2013 Started with a mission to empower women through entrepreneurship in Chennai
  2. Launched WENBA Marketplace Created India's dedicated platform for women-owned native businesses
  3. 1,000+ Beneficiaries Today Network spanning Chennai and beyond, connecting women to corporate buyers
