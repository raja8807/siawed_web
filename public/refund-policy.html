SIAWED Membership Refund Policy SIAWED is committed to fair and transparent membership practices. This Refund Policy sets out the circumstances under which membership fees may be refunded, the process for requesting refunds, and the timelines involved. Please read this policy before registering.

1Overview

SIAWED membership fees are generally non-refundable once a membership has been activated and benefits accessed. However, we offer a time-limited refund window and make exceptions for specific circumstances outlined in this policy. All refund decisions by SIAWED are final.

Quick Summary Full refund within 7 days if no benefits used → Partial refund within 30 days → No refund after 30 days (except exceptional circumstances). Event fees are separate from membership fees.

2Refund Schedule

Cancellation Timeframe Refund Amount Conditions
Within 7 days of payment 100% refund No membership benefits accessed, no events attended, WENBA not activated
8 – 30 days after payment 50% refund Limited benefit use only (e.g., received welcome kit but no events attended)
31 – 90 days after payment 25% refund Exceptional circumstances only (documented medical, bereavement, or relocation outside India)
After 90 days No refund Membership year considered fully used
Declined application 100% refund Application rejected by SIAWED during verification (within 10 working days of payment)
Technical failure 100% refund Payment processed but membership not activated due to platform error

All refund amounts are exclusive of payment gateway charges (typically 2–3%), which are non-recoverable by SIAWED.

3Refund Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Submit a written refund request to info@siawed.org within the applicable refund window.
  • Provide your full name, registered email address, membership ID, and reason for cancellation.
  • For exceptional circumstance claims (31–90 days), provide supporting documentation (e.g., medical certificate, death certificate of immediate family, proof of relocation).
  • Not have been suspended or terminated for a Code of Conduct violation.

4Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refund under any circumstances:

  • Membership fees where the member has been terminated for a breach of Terms & Conditions.
  • Event registration fees once the event has taken place (see Section 8 for event cancellations by SIAWED).
  • Upgrade fees paid to move to a higher-tier plan, once the upgraded benefits have been accessed.
  • Merchandise, printed materials, or physical kits delivered to the member.
  • Fees paid for third-party workshops, training, or certifications arranged by SIAWED (refund terms governed by the respective provider).
  • WENBA listing fees (governed by WENBA's separate fee policy).
Note on Renewal Fees Renewal fees follow the same schedule as above, measured from the renewal payment date. If you have accessed any renewed-year benefit, the 50% window applies.

5How to Request a Refund

  1. Email info@siawed.org with the subject line: "Refund Request – [Your Membership ID]"
  2. Include the following in your email:
    • Full name and registered email address
    • Membership plan and payment date
    • Reason for cancellation
    • Supporting documents (for exceptional claims)
    • Bank account details for refund transfer (account name, account number, IFSC code)
  3. Our team will acknowledge your request within 2 working days.
  4. A decision will be communicated within 7 working days of receiving all required information.

6Refund Processing Timeline

Stage Timeline
Acknowledgement of request 2 working days
Review and decision 7 working days from complete request
Refund initiation (approved claims) 3 working days after approval
Credit to bank account / original payment method 5–10 working days after initiation (depends on your bank)

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method where possible. If that is not feasible (e.g., the card has expired), a NEFT bank transfer will be made to the details provided.

7Renewal Refunds

If you accidentally renew your membership and have not accessed any renewed-year benefits, you may request a full refund within 48 hours of the renewal payment. Requests made after 48 hours of renewal will follow the standard refund schedule in Section 2.

8Event Cancellations by SIAWED

If SIAWED cancels a paid event or program for which you have separately registered:

  • Cancelled by SIAWED: 100% refund of the event fee within 10 working days.
  • Rescheduled by SIAWED: you may choose to attend the rescheduled date or receive a 100% refund.
  • Participant cancellation (before event): refunds are subject to the event's individual cancellation policy, which will be stated at time of registration.

9Plan Changes (Upgrades & Downgrades)

9.1 Upgrades

When upgrading to a higher plan mid-year, you pay only the pro-rated difference. If you request a downgrade after an upgrade, the upgrade fee is non-refundable if any upgraded benefits were accessed.

9.2 Downgrades

Plan downgrades take effect at the start of the next Membership Year. No refund is given for the difference in fees during the current year. You will continue to receive current-plan benefits until renewal.

10Dispute Resolution

If you are unhappy with a refund decision, you may escalate to the SIAWED President's office within 14 days of the decision by emailing info@siawed.org with the subject line "Refund Dispute – [Membership ID]". The President's decision is final and binding. Persistent disputes will be subject to the dispute resolution provisions in SIAWED's Terms & Conditions.
