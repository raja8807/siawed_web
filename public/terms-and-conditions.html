1Definitions

  • "SIAWED" refers to the Southern Industrial Academy for Women Entrepreneurs Development, its officers, staff, and authorised representatives.
  • "Member" refers to any individual, group, or organisation that has completed SIAWED's registration process and paid the applicable membership fee.
  • "Membership Plan" refers to one of the five tiered membership categories offered by SIAWED.
  • "WENBA" refers to the Women Entrepreneurs Native Business Association, SIAWED's associated marketplace platform at wenba.in.
  • "Membership Year" means a period of twelve (12) calendar months from the date of successful registration.
  • "Portal" refers to SIAWED's official website (www.siawed.org) and any associated member login system.

2Eligibility

SIAWED membership is open to the following, subject to plan-specific requirements:

  • Women who are Indian citizens or residents aged 18 years and above.
  • Students enrolled in recognised colleges or universities (Early Explorer plan only).
  • Registered businesses, self-help groups (SHGs), or artisan collectives owned or led by women.
  • Indian-registered companies applying for the Corporate Partner plan.
Note SIAWED reserves the right to verify eligibility at any point. Applications that do not meet eligibility criteria may be declined or cancelled, with a full refund of fees paid.

4Registration Process

  1. Complete the online application form at www.siawed.org/membership with accurate, truthful information.
  2. Upload required supporting documents (proof of identity, business registration, or student ID as applicable).
  3. Pay the applicable membership fee through the payment gateway.
  4. Receive confirmation of membership via email within 3–5 working days of successful verification.

Membership is not confirmed until payment is received and documents are verified. Submitting false or misleading information will result in immediate cancellation without refund.

5Fees & Payment

5.1 Payment

All membership fees are payable in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of applicable GST. Payments may be made via credit/debit card, UPI, net banking, or other methods available on the Portal.

5.2 Renewal

Memberships are valid for one year from the date of activation. SIAWED will send renewal reminders 30 days before expiry. Failure to renew will result in loss of member benefits and WENBA access.

5.3 Upgrades

Members may upgrade to a higher plan at any time by paying the difference in fees pro-rated to the remaining membership period. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

Important Membership fees are non-transferable. A membership cannot be transferred to another individual or entity under any circumstances.

6Member Conduct

All members are expected to uphold SIAWED's values of integrity, inclusivity, and empowerment. Members must not:

  • Use SIAWED's name, logo, or membership status to make false, misleading, or defamatory claims.
  • Engage in discrimination, harassment, or any form of harmful behaviour towards fellow members, SIAWED staff, or event attendees.
  • Solicit other members for commercial purposes without prior written approval from SIAWED.
  • Share login credentials, membership certificates, or member-only content with non-members.
  • Conduct any activity that violates Indian law, including but not limited to fraud, money laundering, or intellectual property infringement.
  • Use the WENBA platform to list counterfeit, prohibited, or misleadingly described products or services.

Violations may result in suspension or permanent termination of membership without refund.
