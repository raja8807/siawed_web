SIAWED Privacy Policy The Southern Industrial Academy for Women Entrepreneurs Development ("SIAWED") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you register as a member, visit our website, or use WENBA. This policy is compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and the Information Technology Act, 2000.

1Who We Are (Data Fiduciary)

SIAWED is the Data Fiduciary responsible for your personal data. Our registered address is 601 "DEEPA", Choolaimedu High Road, Chennai – 600094, Tamil Nadu, India. You may contact our designated Data Protection Officer at privacy@siawed.org.

3How We Use Your Data

  • Membership administration: processing registration, issuing certificates, managing renewals.
  • Service delivery: granting access to events, training programs, and the WENBA marketplace.
  • Communication: sending newsletters, event invitations, policy updates, and member resources.
  • Marketing: featuring members in case studies, success stories, and social media (with consent).
  • Analytics: understanding how members use our platforms to improve services.
  • Legal compliance: meeting obligations under Indian law including GST, PMLA, and regulatory requirements.
  • Safety: detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud or abuse.

5Data Sharing

SIAWED does not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers: payment gateways, email platforms, cloud hosting, and analytics tools — under strict data processing agreements.
  • WENBA platform: your business name, product listings, and contact information are visible to registered corporate buyers and consumers when you opt in.
  • Event co-organisers: name and contact details may be shared with partners conducting SIAWED-affiliated events you register for.
  • Government and regulators: when required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
Your Control You may opt out of event partner sharing and WENBA marketplace visibility at any time by contacting us. Opting out of WENBA visibility does not cancel your membership.

7Your Rights Under DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: raise a complaint with our Data Protection Officer.
  • Right to Nominate: nominate another individual to exercise rights on your behalf in the event of death or incapacity.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@siawed.org with your registered name and membership ID. We will respond within 30 days.

9Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted via our website and Portal.
  • Secure, access-controlled cloud storage with audit logs.
  • Payment processing via PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways — SIAWED does not store card details.
  • Regular security assessments and staff training on data protection.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no digital system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you as required under the DPDP Act.

10Children's Data

SIAWED membership is not available to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a member is under 18, we will cancel the account and delete related data.

11Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites including WENBA, partner organisations, and payment gateways. SIAWED is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

12Changes to This Policy

SIAWED may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered members at least 14 days before taking effect. The "Effective Date" at the top of this document indicates the last revision.
